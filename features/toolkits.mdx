## Browsing Toolkits

### Toolkit Catalog

The toolkit page displays a grid of available courses. Each card shows:

- **Cover Image**: Visual representation of the course
- **Category Badge**: Topic area (Career, Skills, Interview Prep, etc.)
- **Creator Name**: Author or instructor
- **Title**: Course name
- **Description**: Brief overview of what you'll learn
- **Lesson Count**: Number of video/article lessons
- **Total Duration**: Estimated time to complete
- **Price**: Current price in INR
- **Original Price** (if on sale): Strikethrough showing discount
- **Sale Badge**: Indicates active discounts

### Filtering by Category

Click on category badges to filter toolkits:

- **All**: View all available toolkits
- **Career**: General career development and planning
- **Skills**: Technical and soft skills training
- **Interview Prep**: Mock interviews and question banks
- **Resume**: Resume writing and optimization
- **Salary Negotiation**: Compensation discussion strategies
- **LinkedIn**: Profile optimization and networking
- **Networking**: Building professional relationships

<Note>
  Selected categories are highlighted in dark gray. Click again to deselect.
</Note>

## Accessing Purchased Toolkits

### Viewing Content

Once you've purchased a toolkit:

1. Navigate to the toolkit detail page
2. Click "Access Content" (desktop sidebar or mobile bottom bar)
3. You're taken to the **Content Page** with:
   - **Lesson Sidebar**: Clickable list of all lessons
   - **Main Content Area**: Video player or article reader
   - **Progress Tracking**: Checkmarks for completed lessons

### Video Lessons

**Bunny CDN Player**:
- High-quality streaming with adaptive bitrate
- Playback controls (play/pause, seek, volume)
- Fullscreen mode
- Progress auto-saved

**Features**:
- Resume from where you left off
- Click "Mark as Complete" to track progress

### Article Lessons

**HTML Renderer**:
- Rich text formatting with headings, lists, and images
- Code blocks with syntax highlighting (if applicable)
- Embedded media (images, diagrams)

**Features**:
- Printable view for offline reading
- "Mark as Complete" button

### Progress Tracking

Your progress is automatically saved:
- **Completed lessons** show a green checkmark
- **Sidebar progress bar** displays completion percentage
- **Resume learning**: The content page remembers your last viewed lesson

<Tip>
  Complete lessons in order for the best learning experience, but you can jump to any lesson at any time.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do toolkits expire after purchase?">
    No! All toolkit purchases include **lifetime access**. You can view the content as many times as you want, forever.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Refund policies vary by toolkit. Generally, digital content is non-refundable after purchase. Contact support if you have issues accessing content.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Are toolkits updated?">
    Some creators update their toolkits with new lessons or revised content. As a purchaser, you automatically get access to updates at no extra cost.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I share my toolkit access with friends?">
    No. Toolkit purchases are tied to your individual account. Sharing access violates the terms of service and may result in account suspension.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I can't afford a toolkit?">
    - Look for coupon codes on our social media channels
    - Wait for seasonal sales (usually during student admission periods)
    - Check if your college has a partnership program with FTB Hustle
    - Some free resources are available in the Ungatekeep section
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I get a certificate of completion?">
    Certificates are not currently offered for toolkits. However, you can showcase your completed skills on your resume or LinkedIn profile.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
