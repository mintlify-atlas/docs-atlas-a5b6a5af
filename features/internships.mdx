## Browsing Internships

### The Internship Feed

Each internship card displays:

- **Job Title** and **Hiring Organization**
- **Internship Type**: Remote, Onsite, or Hybrid
- **Location**: City or "Remote" for virtual roles
- **Stipend**: Monthly compensation in INR
- **Duration**: Expected internship length (e.g., "3 months", "6 weeks")
- **Application Deadline**: Last date to apply
- **Tags**: Skills and domains (e.g., "Python", "Marketing", "UI/UX")
- **Experience Level**: Fresher, Intermediate, or Advanced

### Search and Filters

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search by Keywords">
    Enter job titles, companies, or skills in the search bar:
    - "Software Engineer Intern"
    - "Data Science Intern"
    - "Marketing Intern"
    
    Search updates automatically with a 400ms debounce for smooth typing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filter by Internship Type">
    Click the **Filter** icon to select:
    - **Onsite**: In-person roles at company office
    - **Remote**: Work from anywhere
    - **Hybrid**: Combination of onsite and remote
    
    Only one type can be selected at a time.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filter by Location">
    Enter a city name to find local opportunities:
    - "Delhi"
    - "Mumbai"
    - "Bangalore"
    
    The location filter works with partial matches.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Clear All Filters">
    Click "Clear All Filters" to reset and view the complete feed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Filter selections are saved in the URL, allowing you to bookmark specific searches or share them with friends.
</Note>

## Viewing Internship Details

Click any internship card to see:

### Full Job Description
- Detailed role responsibilities
- Required skills and qualifications
- Preferred qualifications (nice-to-haves)

### Company Information
- Organization name and industry
- Hiring manager details (if available)
- Company website or social media links

### Application Details
- Direct application link
- Deadline and expected start date
- Selection process (if mentioned)

### Compensation & Benefits
- Monthly stipend amount
- Additional perks (if any)
- Pre-placement offer (PPO) potential

## Saving to Tracker

Manage your internship applications efficiently:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Bookmark the Internship">
    Click the **Bookmark** icon on the internship card. The internship is instantly saved to your Tracker.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Application Status">
    Open your Tracker to update the status:
    - **Not Applied**: Saved but not yet applied
    - **Draft**: Application in progress
    - **Applied**: Submitted application
    - **Shortlisted**: Received a call or interview invite
    - **Rejected**: Not selected
    - **Selected**: Offer received
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Notes">
    Click on the internship in your Tracker to add:
    - Application notes
    - Interview preparation tips
    - Contact person details
    - Important deadlines
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You must be logged in to save internships to your tracker.
</Warning>

## Applying to Internships

### Application Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Apply'">
    On the internship detail page, click the application link to be redirected to the company's career portal or application form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Tracker Status">
    Return to FTB Hustle and update your Tracker status to "Applied" with the application date.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Progress">
    As you receive updates (interview calls, rejections, offers), update the status in your Tracker.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Results">
    Once you receive a final decision, mark the internship as "Selected" or "Rejected" and optionally add notes about your experience.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are all internships paid?">
    Most internships on FTB Hustle offer stipends. The stipend amount is displayed on each card. Some roles may offer performance-based incentives or unpaid learning opportunities - always check the description.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I apply to internships outside my city?">
    Yes! Many companies offer remote internships. Use the "Remote" filter to find location-independent roles. For onsite roles, check if the company provides relocation assistance or accommodation.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How often are new internships posted?">
    The feed is updated daily with new listings. We recommend checking at least 2-3 times per week, especially during peak hiring seasons (July-August and January-February).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between bookmarking here and on the company site?">
    Bookmarking on FTB Hustle adds the internship to your centralized Tracker, where you can manage all applications in one place, track deadlines, and view analytics on your success rate. Company sites only save the listing on their platform.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I submit an internship listing?">
    Currently, internship submissions are limited to verified organizations and admins. If you know of a great opportunity, contact our team via the feedback widget.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
